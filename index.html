How to poll


You can easily create a poll by just following these steps which will take only 1-2 minute :-
  1. There is an create a poll button you have to click on the button


  2. If you are a new user then you have to create an account first then you are able to poll


  3. After register you have to write your question in the question box


  4. Now give options to the user so that they can choose any option


  5. After that click on copy link to share button , you have to press it to copy the link


  6. Now you can share your poll with your friends


Share the poll within your friends


You can easily share the poll in any group or to your friend by just following these steps

  1. Click on the Create a poll button given at the top


  2. After clicking write your question that you want to ask


  3. After writing question ypu have to give the options so that user can select any option


  4. Now after that there is a copy link to share button through which you can share the link pf the poll


  5. New user can redirected to the register page after that they are able to choose any option and give their feedback
