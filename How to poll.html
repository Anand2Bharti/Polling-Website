How to poll



You can easily create a poll by just following these steps which will take only 1-2 minute :-



  1. There is an create a poll button you have to click on the button



  2. If you are a new user then you have to create an account first then you are able to poll



  3. After register you have to write your question in the question box



  4. Now give options to the user so that they can choose any option



  5. After that click on copy link to share button , you have to press it to copy the link



  6. Now you can share your poll with your friends
